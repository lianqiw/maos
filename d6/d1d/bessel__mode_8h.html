Computes the Disk Harmonic Modes, aka Fourier Bessel modes, for a given set of coordinates and a cutoff frequency. The modes are ordered by their spatial frequency (alpha) and returned in a column-major format Inputs:

  • loc: loc_t structure containing the coordinates of the points where modes are evaluated
  • k_cutoff: maximum spatial frequency (alpha) to include in the output modes
  • type: 0 for Dirichlet (Jm=0 at boundary), 1 for Neumann (Jm'=0 at boundary) Outputs: OPD
